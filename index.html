SORT-OUT

ABOUT OUR APP
Hello.....if you are a student you know how your gallery is! Your photos mixed up with a notes and assigments . To help you select your notes from your gallery, we are here with our app ‘Sort-out’. It can automatically separate academic notes, handwritten or otherwise and thus de-clutter our gallery.It uses machine learning to convert the handwritten notes into editable text formats. It also automatically categorizes the notes into different topics. The added features are a calender, to-do list and other resources make it a complete student friendly app To start out, first we have a sign in page. After logging in, we have the main menu which has three main sections. Inside ‘Notes and resources’ ,when the ‘sync with gallery’ option is turned on, the app gains access to the gallery and automatically detects academic notes and save them into documents. ‘Add notes manually’ option allows the user to select images from their gallery and convert them into a PDF. This is achieved by machine learning, specififcally OCR (Optical Character Recognition).
